About me

My name is Haocheng YANG (杨昊澄). I am currently a Post-Doctoral Associate at NYU Abu Dhabi, working with Taoufik Hmidi. I obtained my PhD degree in 2025 at University Paris-Saclay under the supervision of Thomas Alazard and Jean-Marc Delort.

My research interest is microlocal analysis and its applications in PDEs, especially dispersive equations and hydrodynamic equations. Recently, I focus on the well-posedness and (in)stability of free boundary problems.
